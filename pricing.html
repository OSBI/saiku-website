Pricing

Saiku Analytics is licenced under the Apache open source licence, a very flexible and business friendly licence. The open source software model allows us to build an open, widely adopted platform that reaches a large user community whose members provide feedback and contributions to the product and help us improve it continuously based on their needs.

Open source to us does not mean just free for us. It is a model that gives the end user flexibility and great freedom to innovate using our technologies and redresses the balance between the software company and the customer. To help us to continue to develop Saiku we ask everyone who use and gain benefit from Saiku enter a sponsorship relationship with us.

The choice of sponsorship is completely up to you. We guarantee that all the income we receive through sponsorship is invested into Saiku, allowing us to develop and expand our product range.
