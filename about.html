We set out to build the best project management tool for people like us and we won't settle for less.

We have been building stuff for as long as we can think. From tiny sandcastles to high traffic facebook applications. During the past few years we felt that no project management tool out there did fit the way we were working. After bumping into a ton of people who felt the same we decided to do something about it.

We are huge fans of agile & lean software development, kanban, code reviews, pair programming, knowledge sharing, awesome standup meetings, beautiful typography, lightweight user experience, data visualization, workflow optimization, getting things done, XP, simplicity, distributed version control, feature branches, unbureaucratic issue tracking, buzzword bingos and much more.
